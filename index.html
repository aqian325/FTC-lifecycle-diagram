Footwear Lifecycle Diagram

Envisioning a more circular footwear industry.

Each pair of footwear weighs approximately 1-2 kg. The areas of the circles are proportional to the mass of materials involved in each step of the lifecycle.
The size of the circles representing the breakdown of materials and shoe components reflects their percentage of the total mass of each pair of shoes.

Data from Cheah et al., 2012
